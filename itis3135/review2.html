Review Page of Gopinathan, Mila Client Project

Client Project: Student Portfolio

Design Review

CRAP Principles

  • Contrast - The sites colors provide a good amount of conrtase, making sure text is easy to read. The heading on each page could be more stark though, maybe with a more contrasting color or with bolding
  • Repetition - The theme and color pallete is consistent across all pages
  • Alignment - Although most elements are center-aligned, the site is east to read and breakdown.
  • Proximity - Most elements are isolated on each page, with the navbar staying at the top. On the "About Me" page, their are 2 columns for alignment, which allows the image to take a decent amount of space while still staying aligned.

Page Structure

  • All pages have a header, main, and footer with a common navigation bar
  • NavBar is a common h1 across each page
  • Main pages start with an h2 under the navbar
  • There a footer, but its contains the subjects contact info. There is no info for the designer or designer "company"

Validation

  • Unfortunately, the pages lack the accumulus validator

Requirements

The assignment does have 3 dynamic JS functionalites

  • About Me: Dynamic timeline to show the students career timeline.
  • Work Experience: Button to collapse and open information in 2 different blocks
  • NavBar: Navbar is a dynamic component
